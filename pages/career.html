At G & E Industrial Supplies Inc we believe our biggest resource is our workforce. We strive to build a challenging and rewarding work environment where competent people can develop their full potential and achieve their personal goals, all the while adding to the growth of the company.


Diversity And Inclusion:

We fully understand the importance of having a diverse and inclusive environment at our company and we truly believe in giving everyone an equal opportunity to grow irrespective of their ethnicity, gender, age or disability.


Hiring Process:

Recruitment is based on the requirements of the business plan together with current operational demands. We use an efficient, non-discriminatory and systematic method of recruiting and selecting the right people to fill our vacant positions.
